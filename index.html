PALAVA CITY

Palava, a place designed to raise your expectations of city life, is built to provide premium living, on par with the world’s finest cities.This well-integrated visionary city sprawling across 4500 acres of land is crafted by India's leading brand Lodha, seamlessly blending modern urban living with nature. Nestled a short drive away from Dombivli, at the economic triangle of Thane, Navi Mumbai and Kalyan, Palava epitomizes the essence of India’s next metropolis. The city embodies an international standard of living with meticulously planned infrastructure, world-class amenities, and serene green spaces and waterbodies - ensuring an exceptional living every day.
